## 🚀 What It Is
FreshBooks for Windows is cloud-based accounting and invoicing software aimed squarely at freelancers and small businesses. It replaces spreadsheets and scattered receipts with a tidy workspace where you send invoices, capture expenses, and track the time you bill to clients. Since everything runs in the cloud, signing in to your FreshBooks account gives you the same up-to-date books on the desktop and anywhere else you work. The goal is simple: less time on paperwork, more time on the work you actually get paid for.

## ✨ Key Features
> Everything a small business needs to bill clients and stay organized, without the accounting jargon.

- 🧾 **Invoicing** — Build clean, branded invoices, add taxes and discounts, and send automatic follow-ups for unpaid bills.
- 💳 **Expense Tracking** — Snap and store receipts, sort spending into categories, and keep costs ready for tax season.
- ⏱️ **Time Tracking** — Track billable hours by client or project and convert them into invoices in a couple of clicks.
- 📊 **Reports** — See profit and loss, expenses, and outstanding balances so you always know your numbers.
- 👥 **Client Hub** — Store contacts, conversations, and invoice history for each client in one organized space.

## ❓ Questions
**Is FreshBooks free?**
FreshBooks is a paid service with tiered plans that grow with your business, plus a free trial so you can test invoicing, expenses, and time tracking first. Exact plan details are listed on the official FreshBooks website.

**How does FreshBooks compare to Zoho?**
FreshBooks and Zoho both serve small businesses well. FreshBooks is known for its clean invoicing and time tracking that freelancers and service providers appreciate, while Zoho fits teams that want a broad connected suite of business apps. Which one suits you depends on whether you want focused invoicing or a wider toolset.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
FreshBooks for Windows works on modern 64-bit versions of Windows such as Windows 10 and Windows 11, and it needs an internet connection for its online features.

**Is my data secure?**
FreshBooks keeps your financial information in the cloud with access controls and security measures in place. Signing in protects your books, and current security details are available on the official FreshBooks website.
